Roadside Picnic

A Science Fiction Novel Written by Boris and Arkady Strugatsky



“This is the way it is with the Zone: if you come back with swag, it's a miracle; if you come back alive -- it's a success; if the patrol bullets miss you -- it's a stroke of luck. And as for anything else -- that's fate.”
Roadside Picnic

An Excerpt from an Interview with Dr. Valentine Pillman

Prologue

This interview starts the book off with essentially a reflection on what an alien visit actually would mean for humans, and that the biggest discovery isn't necessarily alien tech, but the realization that humans aren't alone in the universe, and that our existence could be of little importance in the grand scale of things.



🌑 🌑 🌑 🌑 🌑

Redrick Schuhrat, 23 Years Old, Single, Laboratory Assistant

Chapter 1

This chapter introduces us to our main character, Red, who is essentially a guide for two inexperienced scientists attempting to go into the zone to retrieve an alien artifact. This is a dangerous task that has a high cost, which ultimately ends in destroying lives for the sake of knowledge, greed, or whatever else compels you to enter the zone.



🌑 🌑 🌑 🌑 🌑

Redrick Schuhrat, 28 Years Old, Married, No Known Occupation

Chapter 2

This chapter jumps forward by 5 years, where our main character has a wife and child that he provides for by entering the Zone to retrieve illegal artifacts to sell on the black market. During a routine sale, he gets into a predicament that leads to his arrest. He runs, but ultimately decides to give himself up for his family's survival over his freedom.
