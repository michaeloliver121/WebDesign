About Lorebird... Me!

I'm Michael, a digital artist and designer focused on fantasy cartography, fictional worlds, and environmental storytelling. Lorebird Maps is a place for me to explore game worlds through illustration, geography, and visual worldbuilding.

Most of my work is inspired by games with strong atmosphere and distinct environments, with a focus on translating those spaces into detailed maps and illustrated projects.
